# UnclaimedProperty AI — Business Plan

## Market Analysis
- **TAM:** $70B in total unclaimed assets; $3B+ added annually.
- **SAM:** 30M+ Americans who move states annually.
- **SOM Year 1:** 12,000 users (1,000/mo) = $348,000 ARR.

## Revenue Model
- **$29 flat fee** to generate the "Unlock Packet" (completed forms + instructions).
- **Unit Economics:** Revenue $29, Stripe $1.15, LLM $0.20, **Gross Margin: $27.65 (95%)**.

## Competitors
- **Official State Sites**: Free but high friction.
- **Finders**: High fee (50%), low trust.
- **Unclaimed.com**: Legacy competitor, slower UI.

## Moat
- **50-State Parallel Search**: Instant gratification in the UI.
- **Flat Fee Pricing**: Massive value prop vs. contingency finders.
- **AI Form Auto-Fill**: Eliminates the "how do I fill this out?" hurdle.

## Go-to-Market
1. **TikTok/YouTube Shorts**: "How to find $2,000 you forgot about in 30 seconds."
2. **SEO**: "find unclaimed money [state]", "unclaimed property search".
3. **Estate Planning Partners**: Integration with digital will/legacy services.
